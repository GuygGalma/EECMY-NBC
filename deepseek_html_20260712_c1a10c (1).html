About Us

EECMY Nagele Borana Congregation — History, Mission & Vision

EECMY (Ethiopian Evangelical Church Mekane Yesus) is a Lutheran denomination in Ethiopia. Our congregation in Nagele Borana is committed to spreading the Gospel, serving the community, and nurturing disciples of Jesus Christ.

Mission: To proclaim the good news of Jesus Christ and to make disciples of all nations.

Vision: A vibrant, growing, and united congregation that reflects the love of Christ in Borana and beyond.
